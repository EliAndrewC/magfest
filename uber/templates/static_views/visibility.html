Visibility

If you are having trouble filling your shifts, changing the visibility may help. The options are described below:

Members of this department

This is the default. Only staff/volunteers who have been assigned to your department may sign up for these shifts.

Staff who've requested this dept or "Anywhere"

Staff who have expressed interest in helping your department - either specifically or by being willing to help anywhere – may sign up for these shifts, even if they aren't assigned to your department.

Staff & volunteers who've requested this dept or "Anywhere"

Both staff and volunteers who have expressed interest in helping your department - either specifically or by being willing to help anywhere – may sign up for these shifts, even if they aren't assigned to your department.

Staff have a staff badge and have worked previous events.
Volunteers have an attendee badge with a volunteer ribbon.
