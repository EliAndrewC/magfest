What Happens Next

After approval, you will be emailed a link to manage your Dealership. From this link, you will be able to:
  • Pay for your Dealership, which includes the cost of tables and memberships.
  • Register your selected Dealer Assistants.
  • After your Membership and Table payment is received, you may add and pay for additional Dealer Assistants. They may also register and pay their own.
Your Dealership Payment includes your table cost, your Dealer Membership, and any selected Dealer Assistant Memberships. After payment, you'll be sent a link by email to follow if you wish to later upgrade your membership. Your Dealer Assistants will be able to upgrade their own memberships and pay for those upgrades on their own.

Later, you will receive an additional email about your placement within the Dealer's Room.

In the event we cannot accommodate your Dealership, you will receive an email. By default, you will still receive a Membership to Anthrocon, and it will be changed from Dealer to Attending (or Dealer+Sponsor to Sponsor, etc). {% include "preregistration/disclaimers.html" %}
