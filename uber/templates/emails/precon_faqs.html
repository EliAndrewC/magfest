Where can I view a schedule of events?
There are multiple ways to view our schedule:
  • Using a desktop web browser: Our online schedule is best viewed on desktop computers.
  • Using the Guidebook app on a mobile device: Go to the Guidebook website to obtain the app, and then search for the {{ EVENT_NAME }} {{ YEAR }} guidebook. Alternatively, if you already have the guidebook app installed, or have a QR code reader app, just scan this qr code. Make sure to check for guidebook updates periodically through the weekend, as we may make schedule changes.
  • Print your own schedule: We have 11x17 formatted print schedule that will be available in our Festival Operations room. Since schedules are posted outside individual rooms, and are also available online, we will only have limited quantities of the printed schedule available. To be safe, please print your own copy of the schedule if you rely on having a hard copy handy.
Will passes be sold at the door?
Yes. Full four-day passes will be available for $60, and one day passes will be available for $40 for Friday or Saturday, and $20 for Thursday or Sunday.
