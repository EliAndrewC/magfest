Selling at MAGFest

We are allowing Indies to sell items in MIVS. If you wish to do so, the highlights to remember are:

  • You can only sell things that are directly related to your game or studio
  • You must own the license to any intellectual property that you are selling
  • You are responsible for paying taxes to the Maryland Comptroller
  • You will need to sign a waiver form and bring it with you to MAGFest, and signup using a google form.
  • The Selling Policy is at the discretion of MIVS

You can find more information as well as the Waiver form you will need to sign here.

Along with bringing a signed copy of the Waiver form, you will also need to fill out this google form.
